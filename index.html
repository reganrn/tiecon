TiECON CHENNAI 2020

DISCOVERING NEW HORIZONS.

TiE Chennai's Annual entrepreneurship conference TiECON Chennai, is the Largest Entrepreneurship Conference in Tamil Nadu this year from OCT O5 - 10, 2020.

Conversations surrounding the 'new normal' instigated by COVID-19 are now part of our everyday life. The pandemic has brought about a distinct shift in the way businesses are both envisioned and carried out. Discussions and interventions are all moving online, geographic boundaries are becoming increasingly fungible, and entrepreneurs are being forced to innovate. Traditional business models are falling away, giving way to new markets and new possibilities. The COVID-10 pandemic has forced entrepreneurs to reimagine what it means to run a business.

TIECON Chennai 2020 will explore these changes and reimaginations. What would business models of the future look like? How does one build relationships in the absence of face-to-face interactions? How can we integrate resilience into our organisations? In a world of such uncertainty, how do we understand growth and productivity? This year's online conference will engage with changing boundaries and definitions in the business world as the ecosystem grapples with the impact of the pandemic. Join us as we discuss and debate this period of flux at TIECON Chennai 2020 - Discovering New Horizons.

TIE SANDHAI

VIRTUAL TIE SANDHAI 2020- A FIRST-OF-ITS-KIND VIRTUAL MARKETPLACE

Having promoted entrepreneurship across Tamil Nadu, it is now TiE Chennai's responsibility to make sure our fraternity of entrepre-neurs advance their business interests by facilitating a respectful, healthy environment.

TiE Chennai presents an exclusive opportunity to the entrepreneurial community to promote their services or products in this uniquely designed marketplace well-christened TiE Sandhai. Ever since the days of barter to today's e-commerce era, the marketplace or Sandhai in Tamil remains the most sought-after destination for every entrepreneur to exhibit their services or products to find a suitable buyer. It should come as no surprise then that the best place to flaunt your services or products is TiE Sandhai at TiECON Chennai.

This year, TiE Chennai is happy to launch Virtual TiE Sandhai @ TiECON Chennai 2020 -the largest entrepreneurship conference of Tamilnadu with a week long virtual carnival Use this unique opportunity to showcase your work and a chance to reach out to 10,000+ entrepreneurs, aspiring entrepreneurs, investors and industry veterans at Tamil Nadu's biggest entrepreneurial conclave.
